Monkee Mail!

Hey hey, we're The Monkees!

Read the below to find out who we really are. These little facts change regularly so be sure to come back soon and see what our new hobbies, likes and dislikes are. Like, did you know that Micky's favourite food is pilchards on toast? We think that's pretty disgusting ourselves.

Mickey Dolenz

Born: LA, California, USA.

Instrument: Vocals and drums.

As a drummer Dolenz makes sure to keep his hands stay agile and so enjoys improving his dexterity by partaking in handy skills such as crafts and building things. He once built a Gyrocopter right in the middle of his living room, but it was so large he had to have a wall removed to take it outside.

Michael Nesmith

Born: Houston, Texas, USA.

Instrument: Guitar and vocals.

Without graduating from high school, Nesmith enlisted in the United States Air Force in 1960 and was trained as an aircraft engineer. After a tour of duty in the Air Force, Nesmith was given a guitar as a Christmas present from his mother and stepfather, thus beginning his love of music.

Peter Tork

Born: Washington, D.C, USA.

Instrument: Keyboardist and bass guitarist.

Tork has previously taught algebra and baseball at a private school but his true passion lies in music, in fact Tork is so talented that he can play 12 musical instruments, deciding which instrument to play depending on the vibe of the music. He also once worked as a singing waiter.

Davy Jones

Born: Lancashire, England.

Instrument: Vocals.

When not performing and touring Jones enjoys horse racing and even trained as a jockey in his teens; he now holds an amateur rider's licence and rode in his first race at Newbury in Berkshire, England. Jones shares a birthday with fellow Monkee Nesmith.
